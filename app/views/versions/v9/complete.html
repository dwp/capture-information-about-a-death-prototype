Next steps for the caller

They should register the death if they haven’t already.

They can also:

  • check what benefits they can get using a benefits calculator at www.gov.uk/benefits-calculator
  • speak to their local council to see if they’re eligible for Council Tax Reduction
  • speak to Citizens Advice for support and guidance
