The caller is potentially eligible if they:

  • get certain benefits or tax credits
  • are arranging the funeral and it's in the UK
  • were a spouse or partner of the person who died

What they could get

They may get help to pay for:

  • burial or cremation fees
  • death certificates and other documents

They could get up to £700 to pay for other funeral costs such as the coffin, flowers and funeral director’s fees.

There’s no fixed amount that they can get. It’s based on individual circumstances. It will not cover the full cost of the funeral.
